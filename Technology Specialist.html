Technology specialists provide support services for technological systems, programs, and networks. They oversee the implementation, configuration, updating, and maintenance of IT systems and components. In some organizations, they may also perform sales duties, promoting certain technology products or services.

Provides technical supportidentifying, investigating, and resolving users problems with computer software and hardwarefor problems specific to the organizations computer networks and systems. Applies knowledge of computer software, hardware, and procedures to solve problems.

A Certified Technology Specialist (CTS) creates, provides, operates and services network-based AV solutions. They conduct activities that offer the best audiovisual resolutions for the client's needs both on time and within budget. They perform general technology tasks by creating, operating and servicing AV solutions.
