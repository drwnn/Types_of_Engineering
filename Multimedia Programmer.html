Multimedia programmers are responsible for designing and creating multimedia computer products that combine text with sounds, pictures, graphics, video clips, virtual reality, digital animation and other forms of media.

Multimedia makes use of various combinations of text, audio, images, animation, and/or video in a presentation, website or other form of communication. This specialty has become increasingly popular in business, education, advertising, science and technology industries.

Multimedia programmers are specialist software engineers and have the knowledge to choose the right programming language, such as HTML, Flash, PHP, etc. as well as ensure the product can be used across different platforms, like Android or iOS.
