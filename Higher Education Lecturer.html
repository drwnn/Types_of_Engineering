Lecturers teach and carry out research in universities and higher education establishments. Apart from your teaching duties, you should be able to mentor students and supervise their projects. You will be required to attend workshops and seminars, both as a participant and speaker. Developing a curriculum and improving learning methods are also your responsibilities.
