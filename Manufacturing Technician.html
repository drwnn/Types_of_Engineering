Manufacturing Technicians operate industrial equipment, work with manufacturing related tools, and perform work processes related to a wide variety of manufacturing settings. They inspect and test finished products for flaws.

A manufacturing job involves the creation of new products either from raw materials or by assembling different components through physical, chemical or mechanical means. Manufacturing can exist on a large scale for items such as phones, cars, computers and food and beverages.

Manufacturing technicians produce products for the consumer market. They assemble components by hand, add finishing touches to components, and perform quality checks on machine-produced parts. They are employed in industries that manufacture physical products, ranging from aviation industry components to kitchen utensils.
