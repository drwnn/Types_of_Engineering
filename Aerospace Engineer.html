An Aerospace Engineer is a professional who is responsible for designing, testing and repairing commercial and military aircraft.

Aerospace Engineer responsibilities include drafting proposals for new aircraft designs, testing new materials and prototypes for future production and reviewing the results to develop new products and parts for our aircraft.

Aerospace Engineers work with commercial and military aircraft to ensure the aircraft operate as needed. They also work with various materials and prototypes to help develop new aircraft technologies to advance the field of aeronautics.
